Logging Attendance

Every team member is required to log their attendance daily through ClickUp. This section covers where to find the form, when to fill it in, and how to do it properly.

📍Where to Log
Required

All attendance entries must go through the official form at Staff Hub → [2026] Absensi → Attendance Form. This is the only accepted method — entries submitted outside this form will not be recorded.

ClickUp navigation — open Spaces → Staff Hub → [2026] Absensi to access the Attendance Form
📅When to Log
Daily

Fill in the form every working day, no exceptions.

On days off, submit the form with status OFF at least 2 working days in advance. This serves as a formal request to management. A notification will be sent confirming the approval or rejection, along with the reason.

🎯Why It Matters
Context

Attendance data is used for three purposes: official presence records, workload visibility for your lead, and capacity planning across the team.

📝How to Fill It In
Required

Each field in the Attendance Form should be completed as follows:

Your Name — enter your full name as registered in the system.
User — assign yourself (click the field and select your name).
Attendance & Workload Status — pick the one that best describes your day: Heavy, Medium, Light, Standby, or OFF. Selecting OFF will trigger an approval flow from your lead.
Date — select the applicable date.
Notes — on working days, briefly describe what you worked on. For OFF days, state the reason (e.g., "personal errand", "not feeling well").
The Attendance Form — all fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required before you can submit

Monthly Cut-Off

Attendance runs on a fixed monthly cycle with a hard deadline. Once the window closes, no changes can be made — so make sure your records are complete before then.

📊The Attendance Cycle
Important

Each attendance period runs from the 26th of the previous month through the 25th of the current month.

All entries and corrections must be finalized by the 25th at 23:59 WIB. This data is used for the monthly attendance recap and payroll processing.

⚠️
Incomplete records after cut-off will be treated as unrecorded. There is no mechanism for retroactive corrections. Any data not submitted by the 25th will not be processed.
✅
Self-check before the deadline Before the 25th, verify that: no dates are missing, all statuses are accurate, and notes are filled in with enough detail to be useful.
💰
How this ties to payroll Salaries are processed at the end of each month based on the attendance data locked at cut-off. Your pay slip — including any attendance-related adjustments — will be sent to your email.

Working Hours & Overtime

Planaria Studio operates on a flexible work arrangement, with output and deadlines taking priority over fixed schedules. This section outlines the expected working hours and the process for raising overtime concerns.

🕐Flexible Schedule
Policy

Both working days and hours are arranged flexibly. The priority is completing your work on time — aligned with the targets and internal deadlines agreed upon with your direct lead.

There is no rigid clock-in/clock-out requirement, but employees are expected to remain available and responsive during core collaboration hours.

⏱️Expected Hours
Guideline

The standard working arrangement averages 8 effective hours per day or 40 hours per week, excluding breaks. This is in line with applicable labor regulations and serves as a reference point — not a rigid daily target.

🔥Overtime & Workload Concerns
Your Right

If your workload consistently exceeds reasonable limits, you are entitled to raise this formally.

To initiate a review, submit supporting evidence — such as task logs, project timelines, communication records, or other relevant documentation. Your lead and management will evaluate the situation and determine the appropriate course of action.

💡
Note Overtime reports serve as operational data to help the team rebalance workloads, adjust deadlines, or allocate additional resources where needed.

Day Off

Day off is a monthly rest entitlement separate from paid leave. Unused days are subject to a rollover mechanism with a defined expiry window.

🏖️Your Entitlement
Benefit

Every employee receives 8 days off per month. This is your standard rest allocation and is separate from paid leave.

📋How to Request
Required

Submit your request through the Attendance Form by selecting OFF as your status and noting the reason. Requests must be made at least 2 working days in advance.

You'll receive an automatic notification once your request has been approved or declined, along with the reason.

Example ClickBot notification — a day off request was declined, with the reason and suggested alternatives
⚡Called In on a Day Off?
Special Case

If an employee is required to work on a scheduled day off due to urgent operational needs and the work exceeds 2 effective hours, the employee is entitled to either a replacement day off at another time or monetary compensation as defined in their contract.

Rollover Rules — The N+2 Window

Unused days off do not expire immediately. They carry forward for up to 2 months after the month they were earned. After that window, they are automatically converted to monetary compensation.

Month N Unused Days Days off not taken during the current month
→
Month N+1 Carry-Over Still available to use as regular days off
→
Month N+2 Last Chance Final window to take the remaining days
→
After N+2 Auto-Payout Converted to cash and included in payroll

Paid Leave

Paid leave (cuti) is a separate entitlement from your monthly days off. It has stricter limits on consecutive use and cannot be converted to money.

✈️Your Entitlement
Benefit

You receive 1 day of paid leave per month. This is entirely separate from your 8 monthly days off.

📋How to Request
Required

Submit your leave request at least 2 working days before the intended date, following the same process as day off requests.

📏Consecutive Day Limit
Rule

Leave may be taken for up to 4 consecutive days at a time. Following that, the employee is required to work at least 5 full working days before submitting another leave request.

🚫
Paid leave cannot be cashed out under any circumstances. Unlike days off — which are automatically compensated after the rollover window — unused leave balances expire without conversion.

Unplanned Absence

This section covers the handling of unplanned absences — including sick days, personal emergencies, and extended leave — as well as the order in which available balances are deducted.

📌
General requirement In all cases of absence, employees are expected to notify their direct lead (Supervisor) or the company at the earliest opportunity, including in last-minute situations.
Deduction Order — Personal Absence

For personal absences outside of scheduled time off, deductions are applied to available balances in the following order:

First Leave Balance Deducted from your paid leave days first
→
Then Day Off Balance If leave is used up, days off are deducted
→
Last Resort Pay Deduction If both are exhausted, unpaid leave applies
🏥Sick Days
No Pay Deduction

Being sick will not affect your pay, as long as you notify your direct lead in a timely manner and provide supporting documentation as required by company policy.

⏰Emergency Absence
Must Notify

In all cases of unplanned absence — including emergencies — the employee is required to notify their direct lead as soon as reasonably possible.

📆Extended Absence (3+ Days)
Case-by-Case

Absences exceeding 3 consecutive working days, or those arising from special circumstances, do not follow a standard formula. These are handled on a case-by-case basis and require approval from:

Your direct lead (Supervisor)
Management

Salary Restructuring

Effective March 2026, two structural changes to compensation apply to all employees. Details are outlined below.

⚠️
Action Required — Effective March 2026

What's Changing

Two structural changes are being applied across the board. Individual details will be communicated separately to each employee.

Starting March 2026, income tax (PPh 21) withholding will be applied in accordance with applicable Indonesian tax law.
There is a restructuring of salary components that affects the breakdown of each employee's compensation. Specific amounts are adjusted by role.
A personalized breakdown with full details of your updated compensation structure will be sent to your email on the same day. Please review and confirm receipt.
If you have any questions, reach out to your direct lead or management for clarification.
